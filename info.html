The Story

I've always been drawn to understanding how people think, what drives decisions, patterns, and behavior. That curiosity led me to study psychology, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in 2022 and built a foundation in how people process information and interact with the world.

Over time, that same mindset naturally evolved into engineering.

Today, I work as a Software Engineer focused on building and maintaining production systems at scale, managing infrastructure, deploying applications, and solving complex issues across distributed environments. My work spans backend development, cloud infrastructure, and automation, with a strong focus on reliability and systems that actually hold up in real-world use.

Alongside that, I build my own systems, from data automation pipelines and AI-assisted tools to full platforms designed to streamline workflows and remove manual work.

I also founded RLA Studios, where I apply the same systems thinking to creative work, building scalable processes for content production and delivery.

At the core, I approach everything the same way: understand it deeply, break it down, and rebuild it better.
